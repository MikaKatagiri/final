Our Mission

Action with Compassion

>

-Linda Fujikawa

The International Café is an award winning service-learning center of Kapi‘olani Community College (KCC) where students can meet and help people from all over the world. It connects students to community service opportunities, promotes cultural sharing, awareness, aptitude, and sense of world community. It is a place for students, alumni, faculty, staff, and community leaders to connect and work towards a common goal. Service-Learning is a teaching and learning method that integrates critical reflection and meaningful service in the community with academic learning, personal growth, and civic responsibility. Students form connections between the class lessons and application/practice in the community. It fosters multimodal learning and sense of civic responsibility that carries beyond the classroom and graduation.
